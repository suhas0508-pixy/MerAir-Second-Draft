Our story

Fifteen years, one desk

Merair started in Bengaluru with a simple frustration: travel bookings were scattered across five vendors, nobody owned the whole trip, and something always fell through the cracks. We built Merair to be the one desk that owns it all, flights, hotels, visas, and everything in between, for corporate teams and individual travelers alike.

Today we're IATA-certified, a member of FKCCI, and trusted by over 1,00,000 travelers and 50+ corporate partners including names like Jindal Steel and Safran. We still operate the same way we did on day one: a named consultant, direct airline ticketing with no hidden markup, and support that actually answers at 2am.
